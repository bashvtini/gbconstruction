About Us

With over 4 years of experience and a reputation for a high standard of work quality, we undertake all aspects of tiling and marble, from small domestic jobs to larger commercial ones. Services

We have an excellent record of high quality work delivered to commercial and residential customers

Due to our experience and adaptability, we can offer an increasing number of services, including:

  • Obligation free visit/quote: Our service's starts with a no obligation visit to your home, where we can discuss your ideas, survey the room and answer any questions you may have.

  • All aspects of tiling: Kitchen splashbacks, floor and wall tiling, patios, hallways, stairs, pool tiling and much more!

  • Demolition: removal of old tiles/fittings; a complete service is provided including the removal and disposal of tiles, fittings and furnishings.

  • Preparation of tiling surface: plastering, boarding, levelling floors including removal of old decorations

  • Natural stone: We are happy to discuss and advice on any project involving stone.

  • Supply of materials: we supply all the tools, glues and adhesives, waterproofing compounds, sealants that are required for the project to save you the hassle of getting this organised.
